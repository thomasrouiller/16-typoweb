introduction

NORSY™ is a variable and pretty cool font designed by AFStudio

AFStudio was founded by Adam Fathony in Indonesia.

In 2008, Adam majored design in college.

In 2013 he started to learn HTML and CSS so he could create his small business.

In 2014 Adam learned about lettering in college. From there, he started to focus on font creation and today his store on creativemarket.com contain almost 50 fonts, mainly display and titling fonts.
